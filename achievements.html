DECADES OF MILITARY EXPERIENCE

Training and Service Record

 
 
 
 
 

United States Air Force Pararescue

Goggins was accepted into training after failing his ASVAB twice. During the training he had been diagnosed with sickle cell trait and was removed from training. He was given the option to restart the training upon his return but chose not to.

 
 
 
 
 

United States Air Force Tactical Air Control Party (TACP)

Goggins worked as a TACP from 1994 until 1999, when he left the United States Air Force.

 
 
 
 
 

US Navy SEAL

After three attempts due to multiple injuries, Goggins succeeded in graduating from BUD/S training with BUD/S class 235 in 2001. Following SEAL Qualification Training (SQT) and completion of six month probationary period, he received the NEC 5326 as a Combatant Swimmer (SEAL), entitled to wear the Special Warfare insignia also known as "SEAL Trident". Goggins was assigned to SEAL Team 5. In his 20-year military career, Goggins served in Iraq and Afghanistan.

 
 
 
 
 

Army Ranger School

Goggins graduated from Army Ranger School in 2004,and received the "Enlisted Honor Man" award.
